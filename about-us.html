Shakti Brass Products

Shakti Brass Products established in the year 2001 by Mr. Nandlal Bhuva, since then it has shown great progress year by year. We have an experienced team of professionals, Technologist, Quality Assurance Team and dedicated engineers in each manufacturing and exporting process to understand, execute, inspect and deliver products to exceed customer expectations.

We have brought the company Shakti Brass Products under the brand name Zeko. It has a wide range of its products, namely hinges, tower bolts, special tower bolts, bolts & latches, window fittings, al-drops, fittings, swing accessories and other hardware accessories. These products are available in different types, sizes and regular finishes like Antique, Lacquer and S.S. Brush. We have proudly introduced PVD finishes like Rose Gold, Sunset Gold, etc in Hardware segment also.

Shakti Brass Hardware accepts illumination process and the designs are inspired from the blend of Indian style with Italic and French styles.

The team of Shakti Brass Hardware works with high tech machines which produces quality oriented products. Every product is tested, supervised and verified by experienced engineers. We have strong productivity, latest machinery and perfect quality control system.We take care of the quality of the customer

At Shakti Brass Products, we assure you to give superior customer service, quality oriented products & after sale-service at the best price. To serve our customer with one of the finest product range and customer service and happy customer because Customer is the King of Market is our Motto.
